GreenPlate: A Sustainable Food Management System

A project by CS2340 Team 20

Home Design & Arch Functionality

Hello from Group 20!

GreenPlate is dedicated to fostering sustainable eating and shopping habits. The app's design focuses on reducing food waste and promoting efficient use of resources. Through this project, users can actively contribute to a more sustainable lifestyle. The app allows users to track daily calorie intake, create recipes, manage ingredient quantities, and generate shopping lists.
